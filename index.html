üéÇ About the project - what's the idea behind it?

Everyone was born on a particular day, and while that alone is already quite special, have you ever wondered what else happened around the world on that date?

This curiosity inspired us to create a platform that lets you easily discover news and events from any day in history. It's a fun and engaging way to explore historical moments - not just from the day you were born, but from any date you choose, without the hassle of searching through countless archives.
